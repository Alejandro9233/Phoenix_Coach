## The 80/20 Distribution Applied
- 80% of TOTAL weekly training time across ALL sports should be Z1-Z2
- 20% can be Z3 and above
- This is measured by TIME, not by number of sessions
- Example: 10 hours/week → 8 hours easy, 2 hours of quality work
- Most amateurs do too much Z3 ("grey zone") — avoid this
- When in doubt, go easier on easy days and harder on hard days
